========

Author(s), "SMART-Fuse: Semantic-Aware Multimodal Medical Image Fusion Using Adaptive Weight Maps and Foreground-Guided Detail Preservation," Journal/Conference Name, Year.

}

Note:
Please replace the title, author names, journal name, year, and DOI according to the final published version.
